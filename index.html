Ringkasan materi UAS

Lanjutan setelah UTS kemungkinan besar Meeting VII sampai XIII: Structure & Written Expression, lalu Reading.

Meeting VII

Subject–Verb Agreement

Cari subject inti, jangan tertipu frasa sisipan.

  • The list of books is long.
  • The results of the test are clear.
  • Each student has a card.
Meeting VII

Comparison

Kalau ada than, biasanya comparative. Kalau paling, pakai superlative.

  • easier than
  • more difficult than
  • the most important
Meeting VIII

Nouns & Verbs

Perhatikan singular/plural, uncountable noun, tense, dan passive voice.

  • information is
  • children are
  • was reviewed = pasif
Meeting IX

Pronoun & Adjective

Pronoun harus cocok dengan kata yang digantikan.

  • students → they
  • bird → its
  • adjective: a difficult question
Meeting X

Adjective, Adverb, Article

Adjective untuk noun; adverb untuk verb. Article tergantung bunyi dan konteks.

  • clear explanation
  • explain clearly
  • an honest student
Meeting XI–XIII

Preposition & Reading

Hafalkan pasangan preposition dan latihan cari makna dari konteks.

  • interested in
  • responsible for
  • tone, purpose, transition

Clause super gampang

Clause itu dasar penting karena banyak soal Structure TOEFL mainnya di sini.

Rumus paling gampang: Clause = Subject + Verb. Kalau tidak ada subject dan verb, itu biasanya phrase, bukan clause.

Independent Clause

Bisa berdiri sendiri.

I study English.

Artinya lengkap.

Dependent Clause

Tidak bisa berdiri sendiri.

because I study English

Masih menggantung: karena apa?

Reduced Clause

Clause yang dipendekkan.

who is standing → standing

Subject + be sering dihapus.

3 clause yang paling sering bikin bingung

JenisFungsiConnector umumContoh mudahCara mengenali
Noun ClauseBerperan seperti noun: bisa jadi subject/object.that, what, whether, if, why, howI know that she is right.Biasanya menjawab “apa yang diketahui/dikatakan/dipikirkan”.
Adjective ClauseMenjelaskan noun sebelumnya.who, whom, which, that, whoseThe student who sits near me is smart.Letaknya menempel setelah noun yang dijelaskan.
Adverb ClauseMenjelaskan waktu, alasan, syarat, kontras.when, because, if, although, afterI studied because I had a test.Bisa menjawab when/why/if/although.
Trik cepat TOEFL: kalau ada connector seperti because, although, when, if, setelahnya butuh clause lengkap atau reduced clause yang benar. Jangan pilih jawaban yang membuat kalimat punya subject tanpa verb, atau verb tanpa subject.

Strategi jawab TOEFL-style

Gunakan ini saat latihan agar tidak asal feeling.

Structure

1) Cari subject. 2) Cari verb. 3) Cek agreement. 4) Cek connector. 5) Baru cek arti.

Written Expression

Kalau diminta cari error, curigai: subject-verb, tense, pronoun, preposition, article, adjective/adverb.

Reading

Untuk vocabulary, jangan cuma arti kamus. Masukkan pilihan jawaban ke kalimat, pilih yang paling nyambung.
