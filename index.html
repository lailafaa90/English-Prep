Ringkasan Materi UAS

Perkiraan cakupan UAS: Meeting VII sampai XIII, karena UTS kemungkinan sampai Meeting VI. Materinya masih gaya TOEFL, terutama Structure & Written Expression dan Reading.

Meeting VII — Subject-Verb Agreement & Comparison

Intinya: cari subjek utama, lalu cocokkan verb-nya. Subjek tunggal pakai verb tunggal, subjek jamak pakai verb jamak.

  • The student is ready. / The students are ready.
  • Frasa tambahan seperti with, along with, together with jangan menipu: The lecturer, with the students, is here.
  • Each, every, everyone, someone, one of... biasanya tunggal.
  • Comparative: bigger than, more important than.
  • Superlative: the biggest, the most important.

Meeting VIII — Problems with Nouns and Verbs

Noun: perhatikan singular/plural, countable/uncountable, dan irregular plural.

  • book → books, child → children, phenomenon → phenomena.
  • Information, research, news, advice biasanya dianggap tunggal.

Verb: perhatikan tense dan bentuk verb.

  • Yesterday → simple past: took, went, studied.
  • Since/for sampai sekarang → present perfect: has studied.
  • Pasif → be + V3: was reviewed.

Meeting IX — Pronoun & Adjective

Pronoun harus cocok dengan noun yang digantikan.

  • The students → they/their.
  • Every student → his or her (TOEFL tradisional).
  • its = miliknya; it's = it is.
  • their = milik mereka; they're = they are; there = di sana/ada.

Adjective menjelaskan noun: a difficult question, an interesting book.

Meeting X — Adjective, Adverb, and Article

  • Adjective menjelaskan noun: a clear explanation.
  • Adverb menjelaskan verb/adjective/adverb: explain clearly.
  • a untuk bunyi konsonan: a book.
  • an untuk bunyi vokal: an apple, an honest student.
  • the untuk benda spesifik/unik: the sun, the final exam.

Meeting XI — Preposition and Usage

  • at + jam spesifik: at 8 a.m.
  • on + hari/tanggal: on Monday.
  • in + bulan/tahun/kota/negara: in 2026, in Indonesia.
  • Pola penting: interested in, different from, responsible for, prefer A to B, pay attention to, succeed in.

Meeting XII — Reading: Vocabulary Questions

Jangan langsung mengandalkan arti kamus. Di TOEFL, arti kata harus disesuaikan dengan context.

  • Baca kalimat sebelum dan sesudah kata sulit.
  • Coba masukkan pilihan jawaban ke kalimat.
  • Pilih arti yang paling masuk akal di teks.

Meeting XIII — Reading: Location, Tone, Purpose

  • Location question: cari keyword pertanyaan, lalu scan line/paragraph yang disebutkan.
  • Tone: sikap penulis, misalnya objective, serious, humorous, critical.
  • Purpose: tujuan teks, biasanya to explain, to describe, to compare, to persuade.
  • Transition: because = reason, however = contrast, therefore = result, another = addition.

Clause — Versi Gampang Banget

Clause = potongan kalimat yang punya Subject + Verb.

Contoh: she studies, the book is expensive, when the class starts.

1. Independent Clause

Bisa berdiri sendiri. Artinya sudah lengkap.

I study English.

2. Dependent Clause

Tidak bisa berdiri sendiri. Biasanya diawali connector.

because I study English → belum lengkap.

3 Jenis Clause yang Sering Keluar

JenisFungsiConnectorContoh
Noun ClauseJadi noun/subjek/objekthat, what, whether, if, why, howI know that she is right.
Adjective ClauseMenjelaskan nounwho, whom, which, that, whoseThe student who sits near me is smart.
Adverb ClauseMenjelaskan waktu/alasan/syarat/kontraswhen, because, if, although, afterI studied because I had a test.

Reduced Clause

Reduced clause = clause yang dipendekkan. Biasanya subject + be dihapus.

  • The man who is standing there → The man standing there.
  • The book which was written by her → The book written by her.
  • When you are ready, start. → When ready, start.

Tips TOEFL: kalau sudah ada connector seperti when, although, if, setelahnya bisa full clause atau reduced clause.
